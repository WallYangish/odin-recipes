Aeropress Coffee

INGREDIENTS

  • Beans
  • water

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Grind 30g of coffee Beans
  2. Put water on the boil
  3. Place ground coffee in inverted Aeropress
  4. Fill aeropress with 100g of water just off the boil
  5. Stir for 20 seconds
  6. Place lid on Aeropress
    7. Invert aeropress into mug and depress for 45 seconds
  7. drink
